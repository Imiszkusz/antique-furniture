Other Antiquities

Besides furniture, we trade other antiquities as well, such as paintings, photos, carpets, glasses and other miscellaneous items, mostly from the 50s-70s era. However, please keep in mind that our main focus is on furniture.

Our Services

Our services besides trading 50s-70s furniture include valuation of such and other antiquities, their reparation with the help of our partner XY Ltd and - as we have significant knowledge of the era's furnishings' trends and styles -, we can give you a professional advice in house decoration, or deliver a complete furnishing plan if that is your need. We also offer delivery service of the antiquities, at a very modest price for our customers.
